About Me

I’m studying Economics and Management, and to me, economics isn’t just about numbers and money. It’s about understanding the link between human behaviour and the economy, how what people do shapes the economy, and how the economy, in turn, influences our daily lives and mindset. This dynamic really interests me, and it’s what keeps me excited to find new ways to learn more about it and apply it. Besides my formal studies, I’m a big believer in self-learning. To me, self-learning means getting hands-on and immersing myself in real-life experiences. We’ve got so many tools available to us, and it’s important to make full use of them. For example, this website is a result of how I integrate self-learning through platforms like YouTube and tools like ChatGPT to help me grow my knowledge and skills. This approach to self-learning also shows up in my work. In every job or project I take on, I follow the same mindset—learning by doing, using all the resources I can, and always adjusting to new challenges. It helps me think creatively, solve problems more effectively, and focus on practical outcomes.
